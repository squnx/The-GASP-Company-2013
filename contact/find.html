PARKING

Three small parking lots are located on Spring Street between 6th and 7th Street, priced from $6/day (higher during filming and special events). Please note that Spring is a one-way street.

Metered parking is available on Spring Street, enforced daily from 9:00 am to 9:00 pm. Meters accept coins and credit cards for time periods up to 2 hours.
